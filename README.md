    🔴🟢 Ball Sort Puzzle – Ultimate Edition

    The Ultimate Strategic Color-Matching Game

    About the Game
    Challenge your mind and test your logical thinking! In Ball Sort Puzzle – Ultimate Edition, organization
    is the key to success—but only if the colors match. Every ball has its rightful place, and it takes tactical skill,
    foresight, and a clever strategy to sort all elements into their correct tubes. With its clean UI, smooth
    mechanics, and engaging gameplay, it’s the perfect brain-teaser for casual players and puzzle enthusiasts alike.

              Key Features

             The Color Rule: This is the golden rule of the game! A ball can only be placed into an empty tube or on
             top of another ball of the exact same color.
             Red on Red 🔴
             Green on Green 🟢
             (And every other vibrant color accordingly!)

              Unlimited Undo Function: Made a wrong move? Don’t worry! You can reverse your steps at any time
              to try a different path and escape tricky dead-ends.
              Global Accessibility (12 Languages): Play comfortably in your native tongue!
              The game fully supports 12 languages with localized menus and text:
              English, Deutsch, Français, Español, Italiano, Nederlands, Polski, Português, Türkçe, Русский,
              Українська, and Čeština.

              Immersive Audio Feedback: High-quality sound effects accompany your entire journey—
              from the satisfying click of selecting a ball to the grand fanfare of your final victory.
              Cross-Platform Compatibility: Fully optimized to run natively and smoothly as a standalone
              app across Windows, macOS, and Linux.
             
              How to Play

              Analyze: Examine the shuffled balls, identify the empty tubes, and map out your strategy.
              Sort: Move the balls from tube to tube while respecting the color rule. Plan your moves carefully
              so you don't accidentally block your own path. Correct: If you get stuck in a corner, simply use
              the Undo button to rethink your strategy and try a fresh approach.
              Victory: The game is won when every tube is perfectly sorted with four balls of the same color!
